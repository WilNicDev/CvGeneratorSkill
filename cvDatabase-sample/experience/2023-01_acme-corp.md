Acme Corp is a mid-size fintech company providing digital banking services to retail and SME customers.

## Responsibilities

- Define target-state architecture for the digital banking platform, aligning technology decisions with business growth objectives
- Lead architecture reviews and design authority governance for all platform changes
- Collaborate with product, engineering, and compliance teams to ensure regulatory and security requirements are met by design
- Mentor a team of 4 solution designers on cloud-native patterns and API-first design

## Key Achievements

- Architected and delivered a cloud-native microservices platform on AWS, migrating 12 monolithic services and serving 2M+ active users
- Reduced infrastructure costs by 40% through right-sizing, auto-scaling, and reserved-instance strategies
- Improved platform reliability from 99.5% to 99.99% uptime by introducing circuit breakers, health checks, and automated failover
- Established an Architecture Decision Record (ADR) practice adopted across 6 engineering teams
