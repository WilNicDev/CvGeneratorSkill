Orac is a Belgian family-run global leader in decorative interior architectural elements, manufacturing and distributing through a direct and indirect retail network. Orac's European manufacturing model — distributing through a global network of dealers, distributors, and retail partners — mirrors the product, partner, and channel data architecture challenges of automotive importers and distributors.

## Responsibilities

- Identify and resolve blockers preventing ecommerce platform go-live
- Lead design of Orac's Customer Experience (CX) ecosystem (Adobe Commerce, Salesforce, Akeneo) and ensure alignment and smooth integration with the SAP backend. 
- Define lean, pragmatic architecture governance to streamline decision-making and accelerate delivery across Orac.
- Align customer master data management across Salesforce (CRM), Akeneo (PIM), and SAP (ERP) — defining the canonical customer entity, system-of-record ownership per attribute, deduplication rules, and data synchronisation policies to ensure consistency across sales, marketing, and fulfilment systems.

## Key Achievements

- Successful rollout of the new e-commerce platform: migration from Magento to Adobe Commerce
- Established enterprise architecture foundations by defining a simple and lightweight capability map in alignment with the management team
- Built simple architecture repository using Confluence
- Introduced lightweight architecture governance tailored for an SME context, including hands-on coaching of solution architects
- Established lightweight data governance for customer master data: defined data ownership between Sales and Marketing, designated the system of record for customer identity, and documented deduplication and synchronisation rules governing data flow across Salesforce, Akeneo, and SAP — using simple turtle diagrams and BPMN level 1 process flows as the documentation standard.
- Supported Sales & Marketing team in documenting their key processes using simple turtle diagrams and, where strictly necessary, BPMN level 1 process flows
- Supported marketing team in the augmentation of marketing processes with generative AI (content creation, translations)
- Assessed overall digital architecture against digital reference model to identify gaps and define roadmap in alignment with business strategy
- Contributed to the establishment of the digital operating model 
- Contributed to the Objectives, Goals, Strategies, and Measures (OGSM) company strategy exercise
