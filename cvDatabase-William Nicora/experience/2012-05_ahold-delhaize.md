Delhaize Group was a prominent Belgian supermarket chain, which merged with Ahold in 2016 to form Ahold Delhaize Group: an international retailer with a portfolio of supermarket brands across Europe and the US.

## Responsibilities

- Serve as lead digital Solutions Architect for the newly created Delhaize Group Digital Centre of Excellence: tasked with creating a standard digital platform to be deployed across group brands across multiple countries.
- Led the head office digital architecture team — directing technical decision-making, coaching architects, and coordinating architecture governance across the 100+ person hybrid delivery team (internal and near-shore resources) within an SAFe agile model.
- Liaise with supply management business teams to ensure alignment of supply chain processes with online ecommerce.
- Establish architecture governance for digital.
- Ensure alignment of technical solutions with business strategy in close collaboration with group-level and country-level business teams.
- Manage major critical situations (P1/P2) for systems in scope of retail, marketing, and digital.
- Cover entire front-office architecture including marketing automation and retail solutions: POS, loyalty system, payment providers, electronic shelf labels, data platforms, and predictive modelling.
	- Ensure alignment with lead back-office architect (SAP ERP and legacy supply chain systems).
- Lead architecture for strategic initiatives across retail, marketing operations, and digital domains.

## Key Achievements

- As lead architect for the newly Established digital center of exellence
	- Implemented agile architecture governance (Scaled Agile Framework — SAFe), steering architecture governance of a team of 100+ people (hybrid mix of internal and near-shore resources).
	- Maintained enterprise architecture repository for ecommerce to ensure alignment between business online and offline business processes (with strong focus on order fulfilment).
	- Led architecture design for General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) compliance in close collaboration with group and local legal teams — covering data classification, information flow documentation across group entities and countries, data retention policy architecture, and the definition of data ownership and accountability structures for personal data.
	- Led architecture of first cloud adoption for Delhaize Group: use of AWS as foundation for the digital platform.
- Contribution to the group digital platform
	- Define digital reference architecture to steer roadmap.
	- Led architecture of  a standard SAP Hybris eCommerce implementation across countries: achieving a 95% common code base and a monthly release cycle across 4 countries (scope has been further expanded to include former Ahold brands).
	- Acted as interim operations manager for the digital platform 
	- Established an  operating model blending the agile operations of the digital platform with the  the "legacy"  delhaize group IT processes and security practices.
- Managed integration of architecture governance following merger with AHOLD.
- Contribution to the customer Data Platform
	- Collaborated with Belgian data science team to design and implement pilot ecommerce recommendation engines (neural networks).
	- Led architecture for Azure cloud adoption  by Delhaize region, in alignment with Ahold group architecture standards and governance.
	- Led architecture of Delhaize region-wide Customer Data Platform (CDP) on Azure, and initial deployment in Belgium — including the design of the conceptual and logical information model (canonical Customer entity and its relationships to Product, Transaction, Channel, and Loyalty data), and the data governance layer covering data quality standards, metadata management, access control, and stewardship across source systems.
