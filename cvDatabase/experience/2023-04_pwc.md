PricewaterhouseCoopers is a professional services firm providing audit, tax, and advisory services

## Responsibilities

- Serve as Enterprise Architect to transform IT delivery into a digital product organisation, reporting directly to the Chief Information Officer (CIO).
- Led the Belgian architecture team — providing architectural direction and mentorship to a team of Enterprise Architects supporting the firm's digital product organisation.
- Establish enterprise architecture governance and principles in alignment with PwC network guidelines and regulatory requirements, guiding business and technology decisions across products.
- Support Internal  Firm Services process inventory and optimisation initiative

## Key Achievements

- Reduced time-to-market for digital products and improved operational efficiency by embedding architecture governance within the firm's agile transformation.
- Defined AI  blueprint leveraging MIT AI Product Design methodology, facilitating generative Artificial Intelligence (AI) initiatives across the firm.
- Championed the adoption of AI across business units, providing architectural guardrails that accelerated safe deployment of AI capabilities.
- Created a Technology Reference Model aligned with industry standards to standardise technology choices across products and reduce repeated technology decisions — covering approved technology stacks, integration patterns, and compliance-by-design guardrails for the firm's regulatory context (GDPR, AML, KYC) — including canonical data entity definitions and information classification standards that served as the firm's Unified Information Model, aligned with DAMA DMBOK principles.
- Supported formalization and optimization of Internal Firm Services (Finance, Digital, Fleet, HR ...) processes, applying the  IT4IT framework for the digital department (internal IT).
