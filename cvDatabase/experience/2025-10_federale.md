Federale Assurance is a Belgian insurance company.

Conducted an Application Portfolio Optimisation (APO) assessment as part of a two-person project team, using Gartner's TIME model to shape Federale's 2030 strategic plan — delivering a five-year target architecture and a prioritised portfolio of project proposals.

## Responsibilities

- Design IT-fit and business-fit assessment questionnaires and scoring models to evaluate the full application landscape
- Coordinate and conduct assessments with IT and business stakeholders
- Co-create the five-year target architecture for the entire application landscape with domain and solution architects
- Align business application investment strategy and target architecture with Federale's mainframe modernisation strategy
- Translate target architecture into a phased transformation roadmap with sequenced initiatives

## Key Achievements
- Defined a five-year target architecture endorsed by executive leadership, directly shaping Federale's 2030 IT investment roadmap and portfolio of prioritised projects
- Delivered the full assessment on time and within budget despite compensating for architecture team capacity gaps (scope included >200 business applications)
- Acted as interim domain architect for Finance and Customer & Sales, with a specific focus on regulatory compliance (GDPR, IFRS 17, Solvency II, DORA, AML, KYC) - including high level financial closure process documentation.
- Proposed changes to the capability map in light of the deep business application analysis
