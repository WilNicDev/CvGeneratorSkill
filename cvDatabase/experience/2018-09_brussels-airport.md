Brussels Airport Company operates Belgium's primary international airport, combining air transport operations with a large retail and hospitality offer (The Loft shopping centre) and a multi-partner customer service ecosystem spanning parking, ground transport, lounges, F&B, and retail concessions operated by the airport or by commercial partners.

## Responsibilities

- Serve as Lead Enterprise Architect, increasing the maturity of architecture governance through formalised principles, decision-making processes, and review gates.
- Led the architecture team — providing architectural direction, technical mentorship, and performance oversight to a team of Enterprise Architects.
- Report to the Chief Experience Officer (CXO) with a dotted line to the Digital Director and 1 Enterprise Architect direct report.
- Strengthen business-IT alignment by driving digital transformation efforts, focusing on customer-facing solutions and Internet of Things (IoT).
- Realise a data-driven business vision by developing a data and analytics platform and Application Programming Interface (API) integration strategy to boost data accessibility and usability across the business.
- Lead major programs including eCommerce platform launch, SAP migration, and IoT initiatives.


## Key Achievements

- Established a formal architecture governance framework — including architecture principles, solution review boards, and integration standards — that became the foundation for consistent technology decision-making and accelerated digital transformation delivery.
- Defined the Digital Reference Architecture for Brussels Airport and an associated 3-year technology roadmap.
- Successfully launched an eCommerce marketplace within 3 months.
- Led the migration towards SAP SaaS Enterprise Resource Planning.
- Led the airport asset management and predictive maintenance initiative and related IOT track.
- Designed the data and analytics platform information architecture — defining data domains spanning the airport's full customer service ecosystem: passenger and travel data, retail and F&B (shopping mall), partner services (parking, ground transport, lounges), operational data, and asset data — along with information flows from source systems, data lineage from ingestion to analytics consumers, metadata management standards, and data access governance, enabling data-driven decision-making across both airport-operated and partner-operated services.
- Matured early AWS cloud experimentation by establishing cloud governance and strengthening security posture.
- Contributed to the enterprise security improvement program, strengthening the airport's overall cybersecurity posture.
